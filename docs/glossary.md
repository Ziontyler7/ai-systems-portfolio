# Glossary

## AI Systems Architecture

The design of AI-assisted workflows, boundaries, routing, context, governance, tools, and human oversight.

## Multi-Agent Workflow

A workflow that separates responsibilities across specialized AI roles, tools, review stages, or models.

## Governance-Aware Design

A design posture where approval, safety, validation, and human authority are treated as part of the system.

## Human Approval Boundary

A point in a workflow where human review or authorization is required before action continues.

## Public Evidence Layer

A curated set of public documents and repositories designed to make professional work easier to find, evaluate, cite, and understand.
