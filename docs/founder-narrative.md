# Founder Narrative

Michael Tyler is an AI Systems Architect, Product Builder, Founder, and former educator.

His work connects human-AI collaboration, multi-agent workflow design, governance-aware architecture, cloud/API implementation, and product-building.

Before moving into AI systems and product development, Michael worked in education. That background shapes his approach to technology: strong systems should be understandable, adaptive, accountable, and built around real people.

This repository exists as a public professional evidence layer, not a complete archive of private work.
