# Ediogram

## Purpose

Ediogram is a fast-paced crossword-grid builder. Players arrange letters into one contiguous connected word block and test their speed through Solo Sprint or competitive play.

## Product experience

The public launch page presents the core promise: build the grid, connect the words, and compete through a private, high-fidelity word-game experience.

Publicly described gameplay includes:

- drawing seven letters to begin;
- building a single contiguous crossword block;
- exchanging letters through a dump-and-peel mechanic;
- Solo Sprint play against the clock;
- competitive play against another player or Chester;
- local storage and no public-feed requirement.
