# The Village

## Purpose

The Village is a local-first practical-skills learning platform where tradespeople, craftspeople, elders, and other experienced community members can share useful knowledge through focused lessons.

## Product concept

The experience is designed around practical knowledge transfer, including skills such as food preparation, home maintenance, vehicle care, and other hands-on crafts. Its public positioning emphasizes fulfillment, community learning, and privacy-conscious local operation.
