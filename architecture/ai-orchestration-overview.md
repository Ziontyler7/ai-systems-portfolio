# AI Orchestration Overview

This document outlines a public-safe view of AI orchestration as a professional design pattern.

A governed AI workflow can separate:

1. Task intake
2. Context preparation
3. Model or tool selection
4. Drafting or execution
5. Verification
6. Human approval
7. Final output
8. Recordkeeping

The goal is not to automate every action. The goal is to design workflows where AI support remains useful, accountable, and bounded.
